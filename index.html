Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 14, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Daily Tape ("we", "our", or "us") protects and handles your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Local Audio Storage (No Cloud Servers)

We care deeply about your privacy. All your voice diary recordings (audio files) are stored strictly locally on your device. We do not host, transmit, or back up your audio recordings to our servers. Your voice is yours alone.

2. App Security & Biometrics

If you enable Face ID, Touch ID, or a custom PIN to lock Daily Tape, all biometric authentication is processed natively by iOS's secure enclave. We do not have access to, nor do we store, your biometric data.

3. Subscriptions & Payment Data

Our subscription purchases are processed securely by Apple's App Store and managed via RevenueCat. We do not collect or store your payment details, credit card numbers, or transaction credentials.

4. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted directly within the application or updated on this page.
